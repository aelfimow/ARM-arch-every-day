# Day 47

Notes reading [Doc 1](../Doc001.md).

* p.344
  - All `A64` instructions have a width of 32 bits

* p.345
  - `W`: general-purpose register holding a 32-bit word
  - `X`: general-purpose register holding a 64-bit doubleword

* p.346
  - `EQ`: Equal
  - `NE`: Not equal
  - `CS` or `HS`: Carry set
  - `CC` or `LO`: Carry clear
  - `MI`: Minus, negative
  - `PL`: Plus, positive or zero
  - `VS`: Overflow
  - `VC`: No overflow
  - `HI`: Unsigned higher
  - `LS`: Unsigned lower or same
  - `GE`: Signed greater than or equal
  - `LT`: Signed less than
  - `GT`: Signed greater than
  - `LE`: Signed less than or equal
  - `AL`: Always
  - `NV`: Always
